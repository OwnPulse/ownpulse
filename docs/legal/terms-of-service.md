## Cooperative Model

OwnPulse is a nonprofit cooperative. By creating an account, you become a member with the following rights:

- One vote in board elections and policy referenda.
- Access to board meeting minutes and financial statements.
- The right to propose agenda items for board meetings.
- The right to export all your data at any time in open formats.

Membership is free and is not tiered. Financial contribution does not buy additional governance rights.

## Your Data

### Ownership

You own your data. OwnPulse stores and processes it on your behalf but does not claim ownership or usage rights beyond what is necessary to provide the service.

### Portability

You can export 100% of your data at any time in JSON, CSV, or FHIR R4 format. There are no artificial delays or approval processes.

### Deletion

You can delete your account at any time. All personal data will be anonymized within 72 hours of deletion.

### Cooperative Sharing

If you opt in to cooperative data sharing, your anonymized data may be included in aggregate datasets sold to researchers. See the [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.md) for details on anonymization standards and revenue distribution.

## Non-Judgmental Platform

OwnPulse does not validate, filter, or flag substance names in intervention logs. The platform is designed to be non-judgmental about what users choose to track.

## Acceptable Use

- Do not use the platform to store data for other people without their consent.
- Do not attempt to deanonymize cooperative aggregate data.
- Do not use the API to scrape or bulk-download other users' data.
