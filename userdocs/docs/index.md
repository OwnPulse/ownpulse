# Welcome to OwnPulse

OwnPulse is a personal health data platform built around one principle: **you own your data**. Every measurement, every check-in, every lab result stays on your instance. Nothing leaves without your explicit consent.

## What you can do

OwnPulse gives you a single place to collect, view, and export your health data from multiple sources:

- **Track health metrics manually** -- log daily check-ins for energy, mood, focus, recovery, and libido. Record interventions (medications, supplements, substances), lab results, sleep data, and flexible observations like symptoms, environmental readings, or context tags. Wearables are optional; manual entry is a first-class citizen.

- **Sync from Apple Health** -- connect your iPhone or iPad and let OwnPulse pull in data from Apple Health automatically. Sync is bidirectional: manual entries in OwnPulse write back to HealthKit so your data stays consistent across apps.

- **Connect integrations** -- link third-party data sources. When multiple sources report the same metric, you choose which one is authoritative.

- **Export everything** -- download your complete dataset as JSON or CSV at any time. Exports follow the OwnPulse Open Schema (CC0 licensed), so your data is portable and never locked in.

## Data sovereignty

Your OwnPulse instance is yours. Data is stored on infrastructure you control. There is no central analytics server, no telemetry, and no third-party tracking. If you choose to participate in cooperative data sharing in the future, that will require separate, explicit, revocable consent for each dataset.
